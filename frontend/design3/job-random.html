UI/UX Design Vienna, AT €€€ Budget Hybrid Deadline: 30 Jun 2026 Figma iOS · Android Open

About the Project

Vault Technologies is a Vienna-based fintech startup with 40k active users and a Series A closing in Q3 2026. We're rebuilding our mobile app from the ground up — both iOS and Android — and need a lead designer who can own the full product experience, not just hand over screens.

The current app works but feels dated and fragmented. We've done the user research; we need someone to turn 200 pages of insights into a coherent design language. You'll start with the core transaction flow (send, receive, split), then move into savings goals and card controls.

We work in two-week sprints with a team of 4 engineers and 1 PM. Design reviews happen every Friday. We use Figma with a partially-built design system that you'll inherit, critique, and improve.

Deliverables

  • Audit of existing app + prioritised improvement roadmap
  • Redesigned core flows: send, receive, split, savings goals
  • Updated component library in Figma (tokens, variants, states)
  • Prototype for stakeholder sign-off
  • Handoff documentation + redlines

What We're Looking For

Strong portfolio showing end-to-end product work — not just UI polish. Experience with mobile design patterns (iOS HIG and Material 3). Comfortable working directly with engineers in Figma. Bonus if you've worked in a regulated industry (finance, health) and understand data density challenges.
